  <project_name>Houston Web Services Portfolio</project_name>

  <overview>
    A professional portfolio website for a freelance web designer/developer targeting small businesses in the west Houston area (Energy Corridor, Katy, Memorial). The site serves as the primary marketing and lead generation tool for the business, designed to establish credibility, attract inbound leads through local SEO, communicate services and pricing transparently, provide easy contact methods, and showcase portfolio work. The target audience includes small business owners in west Houston (77077 and surrounding areas) such as service businesses (plumbers, HVAC, landscapers), local retail, restaurants, and professional services with budgets ranging from $500-$3,000. The site emphasizes a professional but approachable voice, clean minimal design with generous whitespace, mobile-first responsive design, and fast-loading performance with Lighthouse scores of 95+.
  </overview>
