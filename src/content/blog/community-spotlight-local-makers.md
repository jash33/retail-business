## The Heart of Our Community

Behind every product we carry is a person with a story. This month, we're shining a light on the incredible makers who bring passion and purpose to their craft.

### Featured Maker: Maria's Ceramics

Maria started throwing pottery in her garage five years ago. Today, her hand-glazed pieces grace homes across the country.

"Every piece I make carries a bit of my soul," Maria shares. "When someone chooses my work for their home, it feels like they're choosing to share that with me."

### Featured Maker: Elm Street Textiles

What began as a hobby during lockdown has grown into a thriving weaving studio. The team at Elm Street creates stunning textiles using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

## Why Local Matters

Supporting local makers isn't just good for the economy—it's essential for preserving craftsmanship and building stronger communities.

### The Ripple Effect

When you buy from a local maker:
- **Jobs stay local** - Supporting families in your community
- **Skills are preserved** - Traditional techniques live on
- **Carbon footprint shrinks** - Less shipping, less waste
- **Stories continue** - Every purchase writes the next chapter
