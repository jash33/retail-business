The Premium Canvas Tote is our flagship everyday bag, designed for those who appreciate quality craftsmanship and timeless style. Each bag is individually handcrafted using traditional techniques that have stood the test of time.

- **Premium Waxed Canvas**: Water-resistant and develops beautiful patina over time
- **Genuine Leather Handles**: Comfortable grip and exceptional durability
