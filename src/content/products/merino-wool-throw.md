## About This Product

The Merino Wool Throw Blanket is the epitome of sustainable luxury. Woven from the finest ethically-sourced merino wool, this blanket offers unparalleled softness while maintaining durability for years of use.

### Features

- **100% Merino Wool**: Premium grade, ethically sourced fibers
- **Temperature Regulating**: Keeps you warm in winter, cool in summer
- **Naturally Hypoallergenic**: Resistant to dust mites and mold
- **Undyed Natural Colors**: No synthetic dyes, just pure wool tones
- **Generous Size**: Perfect for snuggling on the couch or layering on beds

### Sustainability

Our merino wool is sourced from farms that practice ethical animal husbandry and sustainable land management. The wool is processed without harsh chemicals, and the natural colors mean no synthetic dyes enter the environment.

### Care Instructions

- Dry clean recommended
- Can be hand-washed in cool water with wool-safe detergent
- Lay flat to dry
- Store with cedar to prevent moths
