## Summer Artisan Market

Welcome to our annual Summer Artisan Market! Join us for a day celebrating local craftsmanship, creativity, and community.

### Featured Vendors

Our carefully curated market brings together Houston's finest artisans:

**Handcrafted Goods**
- Leather accessories
- Handmade jewelry
- Pottery and ceramics
- Woodworking

**Home & Lifestyle**
- Natural candles
- Artisan soaps
- Home textiles
- Plant arrangements

**Art & Design**
- Original artwork
- Photography prints
- Handmade cards
- Custom illustrations

### Live Demonstrations

Watch artisans at work throughout the day:
- 10:00 AM - Leather tooling demonstration
- 11:30 AM - Pottery wheel throwing
- 1:00 PM - Jewelry making basics
- 2:30 PM - Calligraphy workshop
- 4:00 PM - Candle making process

### Food & Drinks

- Local food trucks
- Fresh lemonade stand
- Coffee cart
- Ice cream vendor

### Family Activities

- Kids' craft corner (free)
- Face painting
- Live music
- Photo booth

### Event Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00 AM | Market Opens |
| 10:00 AM | First Demo |
| 12:00 PM | Live Music Begins |
| 1:00 PM | Kids' Craft Hour |
| 4:00 PM | Final Demo |
| 5:00 PM | Market Closes |

### Parking

Free parking available in the lot behind our building. Street parking also available on surrounding streets.

Bring the whole family and discover your new favorite local makers!
