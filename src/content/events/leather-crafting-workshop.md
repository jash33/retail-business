## Workshop Overview

Join master craftsman James Rivera for an immersive leather crafting experience. This four-hour workshop will guide you through creating your very own handcrafted leather wallet from start to finish.

### What You'll Learn

- **Material Selection**: Understanding different leather types and qualities
- **Tool Mastery**: Proper use of essential leather crafting tools
- **Cutting Techniques**: Precision cutting for clean edges
- **Stitching Skills**: Traditional saddle stitching method
- **Finishing Touches**: Burnishing, edge treatment, and conditioning
