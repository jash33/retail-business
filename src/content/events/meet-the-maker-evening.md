## An Evening with Anna Chen

Join us for an unforgettable evening with internationally recognized ceramicist Anna Chen. This intimate gathering is your opportunity to meet the artist, learn about her creative process, and be among the first to view her stunning new collection.

### About Anna Chen

Anna Chen is a Houston-based ceramicist whose work has been featured in galleries across the United States and internationally. Her pieces blend traditional techniques with contemporary design, creating functional art that tells a story.

**6:00 PM - Arrival & Welcome**

- Anna shares her journey and inspiration

- Live demonstration of her signature techniques

**8:00 PM - Q&A Session**
- Open floor for questions
- Discussion of collecting and caring for ceramics

**8:30 PM - Shopping & Networking**
- First opportunity to purchase from the new collection

- Welcome glass of wine

### The New Collection

"Vessels of Memory" explores the intersection of heritage and contemporary life. Each piece in this collection is:
- Handcrafted in Anna's Houston studio
- Made with locally-sourced clay
- Finished with her signature glazes
- One-of-a-kind
